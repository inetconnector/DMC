DMC brings capable AI conversations directly to your Android device. Run compatible GGUF language models locally, keep long conversations available, and receive responses as they are generated.

LOCAL-FIRST AI
Your model and conversations stay on your device during local inference. No cloud account is required for chatting. An internet connection is needed only when you choose to download a model or obtain external reference data.

LONG, RELIABLE CONTEXT
Deterministic Multiresolution Context (DMC) preserves important parts of long histories when the physical model cache fills. It combines a stable global prefix, recent conversation detail and deterministic replay levels without recursively summarizing your original text. The available context size depends on the selected model and device resources.

MULTIMODAL INPUT
Attach images, take a photo, add text documents or PDFs, and use voice input. Image and audio capabilities depend on the selected model. Responses stream into the conversation while the model is working.

OFFLINE KNOWLEDGE MODULES
Import supported reference packages and enable or disable them as needed. Official classification data is not bundled with DMC; obtain it from the respective publisher and follow its licence terms. Imported modules remain on the device.

BUILT FOR CONTROL
- Local GGUF model management
- Resumable model downloads
- Persistent chats with isolated conversation context
- Import and export for conversations, settings and models
- Camera, image, audio, text and PDF attachments
- Configurable generation and sampling settings
- CPU-focused Android inference

Model compatibility, speed and maximum context vary by model, quantization, available memory and device. DMC is a general-purpose AI tool. Imported medical classifications are reference data and do not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.
